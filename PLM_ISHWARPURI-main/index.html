Who is
Ishwar Puri?

"i to Ishwar" — The journey from the small self to the Infinite.

Ishwar C. Puri is a Perfect Living Master in the Sant Mat tradition — a living lineage of enlightened beings who have traversed the innermost planes of consciousness and returned to guide sincere seekers on the path to Self-realization.

Spanning decades and continents, his teachings illuminate the ancient wisdom of the Masters through satsang, personal guidance, and the living transmission of Shabd — the primordial divine sound current that connects every soul to its source.

4 Principles of
Indian Spirituality

As shared by Ishwar C. Puri

1

Whatever is to happen will happen that way.

2

Whoever you have to meet, for a certain reason — you will meet not by accident or by chance.

3

Whatever is to begin at a certain time, will begin only at that time — neither before, nor after.

4

Whatever is over, is over.
